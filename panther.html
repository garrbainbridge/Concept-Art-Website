Wakanda

In an effort to update Disneys Animal Kindom and Rafiki's Planet Watch I have created a Black Panther area, Wakanda.

Guests will take a train to Wakanda and explore a land rarely seen by outsiders.

The entrance will feature a sign courtesy of the Wakand Outreach Centre and have the Wakandan flags overhead.

Wakanda Name Tag

The name tag will feature the panther from the Wakandan flag, along with the Cast Members name in the Beyno typeface created for the film.

It will also feature all of the colors of the different tribes that make up Wakanda. On the bottom it will have the phrase "Yibambe" in Wakandan, which translates to "Hold fast" and is the war cry of the Wakandans.
