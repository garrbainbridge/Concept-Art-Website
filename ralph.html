Game Central Station

Inspired by Wreck-It Ralph, a new roller coaster taking guests on a ride through Game Central Station.

The entrance of the building is inspired by retro arcades, much like Mr.Litwacks arcade in the film.

"Walt Disney World is a new game that has just arrived to the arcade. Wreck-It Ralph and Fix-It Felix Jr. have offered to take some of the new characters on a tour. But when they realize the track isn't finished the tour turns into a real adventure."

Identical to other return areas from games to Game Central Station

The ride vehicles are three "trains" hitched together. With a show piece in the front and rear. The front one featuring Wreck-It Ralph and Fix-It Felix Jr.

The vehicle itself is modeled after the Monorail trains throughout Walt Disney World

Surge Protector Costume

The costume for the Cast Members is inspired by the Surge Protector featured in the film. Much like a TSA agent they are there to ensure the safety of Game Central Station.
